Snake Game

The Snake Game is a classic and popular video game that has entertained generations of players. In this addictive game, players control a snake on a grid-like game board. The objective is to navigate the snake around the board, eating food pellets along the way to grow longer. As the snake consumes the food, its length increases, making it more challenging to maneuver without colliding with the walls or the snake's own body.
